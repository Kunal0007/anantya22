ABOUT

Anantya is an intercollegiate technical symposium for Under Graduate students, hosted by the Computer Department of PCCoE. It'll be a week-long event from 1st March to 7th March, consisting of a total of 15 technical and non-technical events.


Anantya is being held to encourage students to create, hack, innovative and show their talents. Anantya offers students a platform to learn, grow and earn exciting prizes along the way.
